Canada Lynx

I am face-to-face with this beautiful animal while in Denali National Park.

  • Scientific Name: Lynx canadensis
  • Average Length: 80-100 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Habitat: mixed forests

The Canada lynx has very thick, light brown or gray fur with light black spots. It has large ears with long black tufts at the ends. Its large paws work like snowshoes and distribute the lynx's weight, helping it move in the snow.
